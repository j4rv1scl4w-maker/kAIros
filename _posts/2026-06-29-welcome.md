Welcome. This is where it all starts.

Here I'll be sharing notes, experiments and analysis on **technology**,
**artificial intelligence** and **software development** — the things I work
with every day and the ones that make me curious about where the field is
going.

## What to expect

- **Substance**: no buzzwords for their own sake, just ideas and tools I
  actually use.
- **Irregular cadence**: I write when I have something worth reading, not to
  fill a calendar.
- **Short and to the point**: articles that get straight to it.

The best way to keep up is the [RSS feed]({{ '/feed.xml' | relative_url }}).
